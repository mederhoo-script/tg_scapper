Scraping Information
What is scraping?

Scraping extracts member information from Telegram groups and channels. This includes usernames, names, and other publicly available data.

Scraping Methods:
  • Direct: Gets member list directly (fastest)
  • Message History: Analyzes message history to find members (slower but works when direct access is restricted)
Important: Large groups may have restricted member visibility. The account must be a member of the group.
Note: Scraping may take several minutes for large groups. You can monitor progress on the sessions page.
